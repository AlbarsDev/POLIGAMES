Hangman

The goal is to guess the word before the figure of the hanged man is completed, which would mean the defeat of the guessers. It is a classic game of deduction and luck that promotes vocabulary and spelling.

Combine the letters

Players must be clever to discover the correct word. Once they believe they have the answer, they press the "Submit" button to check if they are correct. It is a fun and educational exercise that tests the vocabulary and mental agility of the participants.

Word Scramble

Participants must use their knowledge and speed to discover the correct words and write them in the provided spaces. This game is not only entertaining but also helps to improve vocabulary and the ability to solve problems under pressure. It is ideal for those who enjoy word games and are looking for an educational pastime.
Spell the word correctly

A spelling challenge where a word is displayed and players must quickly decide if it is spelled correctly. It is an entertaining and educational way to improve spelling and decision-making under time constraints, ideal for players of all ages looking to enhance their language proficiency.
